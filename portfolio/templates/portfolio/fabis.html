The Relleno Royale

$15

Indulge in the grandeur of The Relleno Royale, a Chile Relleno plate fit for royalty. The star of the show is a hearty poblano pepper, charred to perfection and stuffed with creamy Oaxaca cheese, then dipped in a light egg batter and fried until golden brown. The savory and slightly spicy flavor of the pepper is perfectly complemented by the smooth and melty cheese. This regal dish is served with a side of fluffy white rice and tender beans, cooked to perfection with just the right amount of seasoning. The rice and beans are the perfect accompaniment to the flavorful Chile Relleno, balancing out the heat with their mild and comforting flavor. To complete this majestic meal, we add a refreshing macaroni salad to the plate. Made with al dente elbow macaroni, crisp vegetables, and a creamy dressing, this salad adds a cool and satisfying crunch to every bite. Experience the luxury of The Relleno Royale, a dish fit for a king or queen, right in the comfort of your own home.
